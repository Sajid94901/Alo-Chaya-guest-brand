Hassan Choudhury Family & Heritage

Alo Chaya’s roots are woven into a rich family legacy spanning generations.

MM House (1995): Built by the founder; current family residence with chess-tiled courtyard & badminton court.

Mahmud Tower (18 storeys): Constructed on the land of Fatima Mahal — originally established by the founder’s paternal grandmother & great-grandfather.

The Daroga Lineage: Paternal great-grandfather (Daroga) with 12 children — acquirer of Fatima Mahal land & custodian of heritage values.

Abdul Rashid Chowdhury Family: Direct ancestral line maintaining estate management & cultural continuity.

Project Lead: Sajid Hassan — third son of the founder, stewarding the strategic, operational, and intellectual vision for Alo Chaya.

Pillar II: Mutual Crisis Fund

A dedicated percentage of annual revenue is allocated to the **Mutual Crisis Fund**. This fund is a liability on the balance sheet, reserved **only** for staff/community emergencies (e.g., the **COVID-19 Test** scenario).

Pillar III: Rejection of Debt

The **Guild Founder's Rule** is absolute: **No external bank debt, internal loans, or reliance on gambling revenue.** All growth must be funded by retained earnings, ensuring spiritual and financial independence.

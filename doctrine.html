The Visionary: Abdul Rashid Chowdhury (The Daroga)

In the early 1900s, Abdul Rashid Chowdhury — remembered as "The Daroga" — laid the foundation of the Hassan dynasty. He acquired 72 bighas in what is now Dhanmondi 9A, Dhaka, and built the two-storied Fatima Mahal at 19 North South Road, Siddiq Bazaar. His foresight established both the family’s urban base and its enduring ethos of stewardship.

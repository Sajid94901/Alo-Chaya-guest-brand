The Five Bureaus: A "Common Heart"

Our structure is not a "subpar" hierarchy. It is a Guild of five interdependent bureaus, all managed by the Bureau of Harmony (HR) to enforce the "Common Heart" Doctrine.

1. Bureau of Presence

(Marketing & Brand) Proves our "Lighthouse" identity and enforces the "Dignity Price."

2. Bureau of Provision

(Investment & Finance) Rejects "fraudulent" debt. Protects the "Dignity Wage" and "Mutual Crisis Fund."

3. Bureau of Process

(Operations & Logistics) Manages "Floor Efficiency" (Annual Nights) over "Resource Efficiency" (Occupancy %).

4. Bureau of Essence

(Gourmet & Alliances) Manages "Gourmet Doctrine" (e.g., Nobarunbd, Beans N Berries) as a core asset.

5. Bureau of Harmony

(HR & Doctrine) The "Control" archetype. Enforces the "Objective Standard" (ACEM/GLSM) across all bureaus.

The Objective Standard (Our "Why")

We reject the "subpar," "fraudulent" models of seniority and favoritism. In the Ghuraba Guild, there is only one "ground truth": The Objective Standard. All pay, promotion, and authority are tied directly to these two scores.

GLSM (The "Intellect" Score)

The **Ghuraba Leadership & Strategy Model**. Measures your *intellect* and strategic alignment with the "Common Heart" Doctrine. This is the "Why."

ACEM (The "Physique" Score)

The **Action-Centric Efficiency Model**. Measures your *physique* and "Generalization + Specialization" skills. This is the "How."

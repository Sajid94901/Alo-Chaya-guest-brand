1. The Inversion of Authority (The Moral Auditor Mandate)

The Bureau of Harmony is decentralized. The traditional, un-coachable central HR function is stripped of moral authority. All ethical compliance is handled by the HR Moral Auditor at the branch level, who acts as the Specialist’s direct guardian.

2. Moral Purity and Guardrail of Integrity

The Bureau of Harmony enforces the moral purity of the Guild, protecting it from both internal and external corruption, as mandated by the Founder's Rule.

Moral Purity and Right to Eject

Guests are treated as students in the Training Centre. The Guild has the absolute, non-negotiable right to eject any guest engaging in gross moral failures (e.g., exploitation, adultery) that violate the spiritual purity and integrity of the Common Heart.

The Guardrail of Integrity (The Ultimate Test)

We reject all forms of corruption:

  1. Bidirectional Exploitation: Neither staff exploiting upper management, nor upper management exploiting staff.
  2. External Contamination: The Leader's private life must never introduce external moral/financial poison that destroys the Guild.
The Guild Founder's Rule (no gambling, internal loans, or debt) is absolute.

3. The Recruitment & Onboarding Protocol (The Employee Story)

We do not just "hire" people. We begin the Employee Story. Our system is a data-driven model designed for personalization, connecting every stage of the employee lifecycle. This ensures we recruit the right people, grow them as Specialists, and learn from their journey to constantly improve the Guild.

Step 1: Discovery & Application

It starts with our Company Branding and honest Recruitment Discovery. Prospective Specialists apply via our portal, submitting not just a CV, but the raw data for their Employee Story, such as:

  • Full CV (Previous Experience)
  • Technical & Soft Skill Competencies
  • MBA/BBA Course Outlines
  • All relevant certificates

Step 2: The ACEM Assessment

All applications are assessed based on the ACEM Purity Mandate. We test only for skills 100% relevant to the codified job role. We ban all irrelevant academic and cultural barriers, ensuring a fair, merit-based selection.

Step 3: The Trainee Specialist

Successful candidates are not "Interns." They are hired as paid Trainee Specialists at a Dignity Wage. They are given a codified role to begin their journey, allowing them to learn and contribute from day one.

Recruitment Process: The Anti-CV Audit

Our selection process is designed to filter for **capacity, character, and competence** by rigorously challenging paper credentials. We treat the CV as a hypothesis to be disproved, using the **ACEM** as the objective scoring tool to identify ethical and competency gaps.

Phase 1: The Dark Triad Filter

Every application is audited for **Narcissism and Entitlement** (using the **ACEM Individual Block**). High prestige markers (foreign degrees, old publications) trigger increased scrutiny in the interview to screen for the **Vice of Intellectual Arrogance.**

Phase 2: The L1 Generalist Challenge

The technical interview is not a test of memory, but of **synthesis**. Candidates must immediately integrate abstract theory (like Mintzberg's 5Ps) into the **ACEM structure** (e.g., justifying the 88k wage with the Skill Block). Failure to synthesize is flagged as a **Level 1 Generalist.**

Phase 3: The GLSM Vice-Diagnosis

The final hiring recommendation is based on a **GLSM Vice-Diagnosis**. We don't say "under-qualified." We diagnose the core fault: "Candidate exhibits the **Vice of Lethargy**, which conflicts with the Virtue of Diligence required by the Objective Standard."

Onboarding: Process, Action, and OODA

Onboarding is not paperwork; it is the immediate establishment of **process adherence** and the shift from "talk culture" to "act culture." We enforce the **OODA Loop** within the first week to prioritize speed and quality decision-making over academic perfection.

First 24 Hours: Process Audit

The new hire's minute task is to **audit their job process** against the **5Ps of SHRM** and identify one **Mu (Muda, Mura, Muri)** to reduce. This immediately tests and proves their **Conscientiousness** (ACEM Individual Block) by demanding practical improvement.

First 7 Days: Enforced OODA Loop

New hires complete timed diagnostic tasks (e.g., addressing a student complaint) that force them through **Observe-Orient-Decide-Act** in under 60 minutes. This is a direct test of the individual's ability to act decisively, punishing **Lethargy** and rewarding high-quality friction.

Follower Level Progression

All training goals are set using **Kelley's Follower Types**. We bypass generic leadership training and diagnose the new hire's current engagement, setting explicit goals to evolve them toward the **Exemplary Follower** type (ACEM Skill Block 2).

Orientation: Anchoring Ethics and Culture

Orientation is the process of cultural and ethical alignment, contrasting our **Task-Oriented culture** with the local **Person-Oriented culture** to prevent conflict. It is an affirmation of the **Common Heart Doctrine** and the ethical obligation tied to the 88k Dignity Wage.

Ethical Commitment Sign-Off

Employees sign a commitment document affirming their understanding of the **Virtue-Vice Spectrum**. This makes the demonstration of **Honesty** and **Diligence** an explicit contractual and ethical obligation, not just a suggestion.

Cultural Alignment Check

The briefing explicitly contrasts the desired **Task-Oriented Culture** (Trompenaars) with the local **Person-Oriented Culture**. We make the difference transparent, forcing the employee to consciously choose to shed habits that prioritize connections over objective output.

Ghuraba Guild Perspective

The **88k Dignity Wage** is tied to the **Ghuraba Guild Perspective**. Employees are reminded that their sloth or low performance is an **injustice** to their highly-paid colleagues, tying personal ethics directly to strategic financial practices (5Ps of Strategy).

Performance Management: The Character Audit (BARS & 360)

Performance management is the objective enforcement of the **Common Heart Doctrine** via data. We use **Behaviorally Anchored Rating Scales (BARS)** and **360-Degree Feedback** across all levels to replace subjective judgment with objective behavioral scores, making it impossible for low-capacity individuals to hide behind a title.

BARS: Measuring Character, Not Just Output

We use **BARS** (Behaviorally Anchored Rating Scales) to score **Virtues and Vices** (Aristotelian Spectrum), ensuring performance is measured against observable, minute behaviors, not just general outcomes. This ties the **ACEM Individual Block** directly to the performance review.

Example (BARS for Diligence):

  • **Exemplary (7.5):** Employee's daily Kata checklist shows proactive **Reduction of Muri (Overburden)** in their own process, independently resolving systematic friction points. (ACEM Virtue: Diligence)
  • **Needs Improvement (4.0):** Employee only fixes process friction when explicitly asked or when a task is personally blocked. Shows no initiative to audit workflow. (ACEM Vice: Lethargy)

360-Degree Feedback: Auditing the Hierarchy

360-degree feedback is applied at **all levels** to prevent **Credential-Based Arrogance** from insulating any employee. It ensures that an Executive is audited not just by supervisors, but by the people they impact most—their subordinates and peers.

Application (All Levels):

  • **Professor Level:** A Professor's score is equally weighted by their students (reporting **L1 Generalism** in the Skill Block) and support staff (reporting **Dark Triad** entitlement/abuse).
  • **Executive Level:** The CEO's **Authenticity** and **Justice** (Virtues) are scored by direct reports on whether directives are clear and ethically sound, not just strategically successful.

The ACEM Score: The Final Judgment

The BARS/360 data forms the only input for the employee's **ACEM Score**. This score is the objective standard for professional evolution, promotion, and termination, making subjective managerial preference irrelevant.

Final Integration:

  • **Pay:** Raises and bonuses are exclusively tied to a rising ACEM Score, reinforcing the Objective Standard.
  • **Intervention:** A consistently low ACEM Score triggers mandatory, immediate intervention via the **GLSM diagnostic flow** (Observe -> Orient -> Decide on a Vice Prescription), bypassing the typical bureaucratic HR warning process.

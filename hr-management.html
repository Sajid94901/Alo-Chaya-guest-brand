Bureau of Harmony (HR) Strategy

The HR function is the **Stabilizer** that enforces the **Common Heart Doctrine** and the **Objective Standard**.

The Inversion of Authority: The Moral Auditor Mandate

The Bureau of Harmony is decentralized. The traditional, un-coachable central HR function is stripped of moral authority. All ethical compliance is handled by the **HR Moral Auditor** at the branch level, who acts as the Specialist’s direct guardian.

HR Moral Auditor: The Branch Stabilizer

Anti-Shame & Anti-Exploitation Duty

The Auditor's sole focus is auditing the **Moral of the People** and process compliance. They report **Dignity Violations** (shaming, misassignment, linguistic bias) **directly to the Governance Panel**, bypassing the lackadaisical central management. This parallel reporting structure ensures the Auditor is protected from **Bidirectional Exploitation** by the Central Head.

ACEM Purity Mandate (Testing)

Objective Standard Guarantee

The **ACEM Score** is the only metric for pay and promotion. **ACEM Purity** is enforced: Testing must be 100% relevant to the codified job duties. Irrelevant academic knowledge (geometry, algebra) and cultural gatekeeping ("Bangla Boli") are **banned** from all assessments, protecting the Specialist from archaic, unfair barriers.

Trainee Specialist (Dab Lok Doctrine)

Dignified Delegation Layer

The term "Intern" is banned. The **Trainee Specialist** is a paid rank receiving the **Dignity Wage**. Their job is codified: handle foundational administrative duties, allowing MBA/BBA Specialists to focus on strategic **Producer** roles. This eliminates the exploitation recorded in the work logs (Day 18-19).

The Referral Cost Doctrine

Accountability in Talent Acquisition

To combat lazy hiring, the **Referral Cost** doctrine is active: any Specialist who refers a hire that fails to meet their **ACEM Score** metrics incurs a penalty in their own ACEM Score and Performance Bonus. This forces the system to **Hire the Right People** and take ownership of candidate quality.

The ACEM Linkage Doctrine (HRIS as Central Data Core)

The Bureau of Harmony centrally administers the **ACEM Score**, which functions as the objective data core for all departmental functions. This prevents subjective managerial bias from corrupting data regarding compensation, training, or performance.

Linkage 1: Compensation & Payroll

Compensation Purity

The **ACEM Score** automatically dictates **Step Payments** (pay raises). HR’s payroll function is mandated to pull ACEM data directly; no managerial approval can override a raise triggered by a verified increase in a Specialist's ACEM Score.

Linkage 2: Operations & T&D

Operational Efficiency

The **ACEM Score** identifies organizational skill deficits, informing the **Operations Bureau’s** **Permanent Power Planning**. HR administers the **T&D Gateway** and tracks Trainee/Specialist progress, ensuring that only ACEM-certified personnel are deployed for specific tasks.

Linkage 3: CPM (Customer Feedback)

Accountability Ledger Integration

Guest feedback (from the **CPM Dashboard**) is continuously fed into the **Accountability Ledger**. The ledger cross-references negative feedback with the responsible Specialist's **ACEM Score** to identify training deficiencies or ethical failures, driving necessary corrections objectively.

Linkage 4: Manpower & Promotions

Zero-Risk Mobility

All promotions (e.g., to **Universalist**) and **Zero-Risk Mobility** requests (rotation) are triggered by the ACEM Score's multi-disciplinary proficiency markers. HR’s planning is driven by ACEM data, not by managerial preference.

Moral Purity and Guardrail of Integrity

The Bureau of Harmony enforces the moral purity of the Guild, protecting it from both internal and external corruption, as mandated by the Founder's Rule.

Moral Purity and Right to Eject

Guests are treated as students in the Training Centre. The Guild has the **absolute, non-negotiable right to eject** any guest engaging in gross moral failures (e.g., exploitation, adultery) that violate the spiritual purity and integrity of the Common Heart.

The Guardrail of Integrity (The Ultimate Test)

We reject all forms of corruption:

  1. **Bidirectional Exploitation:** Neither staff exploiting upper management, nor upper management exploiting staff.
  2. **External Contamination:** The Leader's private life must never introduce external moral/financial poison that destroys the Guild.
The **Guild Founder's Rule** (no gambling, internal loans, or debt) is absolute.

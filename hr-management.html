HR Doctrine: The Human Engine

Our HRM doctrine is built on dynastic continuity and operational modernity. We are hosts, not staff—cultivating the future guardians of the Alo Chaya legacy.

A. Recruitment & Onboarding

Local First Initiative: Hiring from the Chandpur community to foster sustainable employment and authentic connection.

Alo Chaya Immersion: Mandatory 7-day program for all new hires, covering heritage, wellness philosophy, and operational standards.

B. Training (House of Light Academy)

Our training is modeled on a "Shokugeki-Style" challenge-based system.

  • Challenge-Based Learning: Weekly operational and culinary challenges with real-time feedback.
  • Mentor-Mentee Ladder: Pairing juniors with seniors for skill growth.
  • Defined Skill Tiers: Apprentice → Journeyman → Captain → Innovator.

C. Staff Wellness & Retention

  • Premium Accommodation & Meals: Safe, high-quality housing and nutritious meals.
  • Access to Wellness: Wellness Center access to embrace all 8 Dimensions of Wellness.
  • Daycare Initiative: On-site daycare & education for staff children.

D. Leadership & Promotion

  • Leadership Framework: Level 5 (Collins), 5 Levels Path (Maxwell), Exemplary Followers (Kelley).
  • Promotion System: Skill/Will (Hersey-Blanchard) + Tannenbaum-Schmidt continuum for objective advancement.

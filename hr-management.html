Hiring: The New Ranks

We recruit based on the "Posture" (Who) and "Discipline" (How) from the Codex. We hire for character, dignity, and a passion for craftsmanship.

Oversight Layer

Roles for the "Council of Bureau Chiefs." These are strategic leaders responsible for upholding the Codex and setting departmental OKRs.

Operational Layer

The "Gourmet Corp" ranks. This is our core team, from Department Heads to Apprentices. We look for technical skill and a 'learning' mindset.

Service Layer

The "Konohana Kitan" philosophy. We hire for empathy, warmth, and the ability to treat guests as "pilgrims of light."

Training: The "Shokugeki" Model

Training is based on the "Food Wars" model of practical, high-stakes challenges and continuous, mentored feedback. This is tracked in the internal HRIS.

Codex Modules

All staff must complete training modules on the Codex, ethics, and the brand philosophy (the "Mirror" and "Posture").

Skill Challenges (ACEM)

Staff participate in simulations and 'skill battles' to prove mastery. This builds their "ACEM" score (Al-Amin Capability Evolution Model).

Soft-Skill Development

Empathy, mindfulness, and calm service presence, based on the "Konohana Kitan" (Service Layer) philosophy.

Compensation: The Performance Link

Our compensation is transparent and directly linked to the two things we value: personal growth and ethical performance.

1. "Step Payments" (For Growth)

This is a permanent salary increase. When you master a new skill and increase your **ACEM score** (from training), your base pay takes a "step" up. We pay for what you can do.

2. Performance Bonus (For Results)

This is a quarterly cash bonus. It is calculated by the **internal HRIS dashboard** and is based 100% on your performance against the **CSFs** (ethics) and **OKRs** (goals).

5. Onboarding & The Guild Hall: The Generalization + Specialization Model

Our process rejects the flaws of over-specialization and over-generalization. Onboarding is a phased entry into the **In-House Training & Wellness Centre** ("The Guild Hall"), where new staff begin their journey of mastery and purpose.

Recruitment: Posture First

We select for **"Posture"** (character) over raw skill. We look for individuals who demonstrate **Faithful Stewardship** in their personal lives, thus avoiding those who are financially reckless or arrogant. The initial focus is on the character needed to be part of the "Fairy Tail" family.

T&D: Mastery & The Step Payment Incentive

Training is built on the **ACEM (Capability Evolution Model)**. It is a dual track: **Specialization** (mastering the rank) and **Generalization** (Dejobbing). The incentive is the **Mastery Step Payment**: when an Executive Chef (84k) masters a new skill (e.g., Math teaching for his kids) from the Office Universalist (88k), he earns a **permanent salary increase** (a Step Payment) *added* to his basic pay.

6. The Common Heart: Standards, Rights, and Duties (The Ultimate Safeguard)

The relationship between staff and guests is built on mutual respect and development. **All are students; all have standards.**

Staff Standard: Dignity, Growth, and Support

Staff are respected as Specialists. Our ultimate safeguard is the **Mutual Crisis Fund** (supported by all stakeholders), providing dignified financial, medical, and life-event support as a right of the family, rejecting all interest-based systems (Haram).

Guest Standard: Moral Purity and Right to Eject

Guests are **students** in the Training Centre. The **Konohana Kitan** model applies: Guests have the duty to maintain a **Standard of Moral Purity** on the premises. The Guild has the **absolute, non-negotiable right to eject** any guest engaging in gross moral failures (e.g., exploitation, adultery, or the "calling girl adult" scenario) that violate the spiritual purity and integrity of the Common Heart.

The Guardrail of Integrity (The Ultimate Test)

We reject all forms of corruption:

  1. **Bidirectional Exploitation:** Neither staff exploiting upper management, nor upper management exploiting staff.
  2. **External Contamination:** The Leader's private life (**Castlevania** analogy) must never introduce external moral/financial poison that destroys the Guild.
The **Guild Founder's Rule** (no gambling, internal loans, or debt) is absolute.

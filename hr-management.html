Hiring: The New Ranks

We recruit based on the "Posture" (Who) and "Discipline" (How) from the Codex. We hire for character, dignity, and a passion for craftsmanship.

Oversight Layer

Roles for the "Council of Bureau Chiefs." These are strategic leaders responsible for upholding the Codex and setting departmental OKRs.

Operational Layer

The "Gourmet Corp" ranks. This is our core team, from Department Heads to Apprentices. We look for technical skill and a 'learning' mindset.

Service Layer

The "Konohana Kitan" philosophy. We hire for empathy, warmth, and the ability to treat guests as "pilgrims of light."

Training: The "Shokugeki" Model

Training is based on the "Food Wars" model of practical, high-stakes challenges and continuous, mentored feedback. This is tracked in the internal HRIS.

Codex Modules

All staff must complete training modules on the Codex, ethics, and the brand philosophy (the "Mirror" and "Posture").

Skill Challenges (ACEM)

Staff participate in simulations and 'skill battles' to prove mastery. This builds their "ACEM" score (Al-Amin Capability Evolution Model).

Soft-Skill Development

Empathy, mindfulness, and calm service presence, based on the "Konohana Kitan" (Service Layer) philosophy.

Compensation: The Performance Link

Our compensation is transparent and directly linked to the two things we value: personal growth and ethical performance.

1. "Step Payments" (For Growth)

This is a permanent salary increase. When you master a new skill and increase your **ACEM score** (from training), your base pay takes a "step" up. We pay for what you can do.

2. Performance Bonus (For Results)

This is a quarterly cash bonus. It is calculated by the **internal HRIS dashboard** and is based 100% on your performance against the **CSFs** (ethics) and **OKRs** (goals).

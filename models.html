Activities and learning crosswalks for Specialists and Guests.

Tier 1 — Nafs (Emotional & Occupational)

  • Burnout avoidance, journaling, financial counselling referrals.
  • Workshops on daily planning & time-energy management.

Tier 2 — Qalb (Heart & Social)

  • Group empathy exercises, family rituals, gratitude practice.
  • Peer mentoring and small-group conflict resolution drills.

Tier 3 — Sirr (Inner Reflection)

  • Guided solitude time, reflective prompts from Lighthouse Tales.
  • Quiet corners and reading lists; micro-meditations.

Tier 4 — Ruh (Spiritual Foundation)

  • Tazkiyah practices, short guided dhikr, spiritual journaling.
  • Optional Qur'anic reflection sessions and moral clarity prompts.

Framework crosswalks: Bloom, Dale, McClelland, Alderfer—all mapped to activities for measurable learning outcomes.

1. The 7Ps: The "Common Heart" Strategy

The 7Ps align our "Fairy Tail" family (staff, guests, and their families) with our Doctrine. This is **how we are a lighthouse to all other resorts in the world**: by proving that "Holistic Well-Being" for *all* stakeholders is the only true business model.

Product: The "In-House Training Centre"

Our "product" is not just rooms; it is **Holistic Well-Being**. The core service is the **"In-House Training & Wellness Centre."** This is a shared "Guild Hall" where *both* guests and employees (and their families) are **"students"** together.

Price: Premium Scarcity

Positioned in the **mid-to-high luxury tier**. Justified not by marble, but by our **"Generalization + Specialization"** model. Guests are *investing* in a "Guild" that provides "dignity wages" (e.g., 88k Universalist) to its family.

Place: The Shared "Guild Hall"

The **Chandpur Riverine site** is the physical location of our "In-House Training Centre." It is a sanctuary where all "family" (guests and staff) can learn and grow together, proving the "common heart" principle.

Promotion: The "Lighthouse" Proof

**This is why you should come to our resort.** We do not sell "luxury"; we promote the "common heart." Our marketing shows the 84k Executive Chef learning *from* the 30k Room Coordinator Chief. We are the "lighthouse" that proves a "Fairy Tail" is a real, functional business model.

People: The "Guild" (All are Family)

Staff and Guests are one "Guild." Both have **"rights, duties, and standards."** Our staff are "Dignified Guides" (not servants) and our guests are "Dignified Students" (not tyrants), all learning together in the Training Centre.

Process: The "Dejobbing" Journey

Our service process *is* the **"Generalization + Specialization"** model. The "Onboarding" process for a guest or a new employee is the same: they are introduced to the **Codex** and the **Training Centre** to begin their *personal* growth journey.

Physical Evidence: Marble & Manuscript

This includes the marble interiors, the river view, and the **In-House Training Centre** itself. The **Ghuraba Al Manara Codex** is physical evidence of the "curriculum" that both guests and staff follow.

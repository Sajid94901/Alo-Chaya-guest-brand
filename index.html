Culinary & Beverage Experience

At Alo Chaya, dining is a journey — where local craftsmanship meets global flavor. Our cuisines and drinks are curated with sincerity, discipline, and artistry under the guidance of the Project Lead, who has been mastering culinary craft since 2013.

🍛 Culinary Experience

▼

Our three signature cuisines — Bengali-Indian, Italian, and Pan-Asian Fusion (Thai, Chinese & Japanese) — are crafted by a professional team with over 15 years of experience. Every dish embodies the Project Lead’s principle: “A leader cooks with his team to understand, balance, and uplift.”

Featured dishes include Golden Basa Fillets, Thai-Chinese Fusion Rice, and the Balanced Wellness Plate — each reflecting harmony between taste and wellbeing.

☕ Beverage & Wellness Bar

▼

Refreshment at Alo Chaya is an art form. From espresso macchiatos to latte macchiatos with morning syrups and house-brewed teas like our Signature Peach Tea, each drink blends indulgence and wellness.

Guests following personalized diet or wellness plans are catered to with tailored options — we suggest, never impose. Every beverage embodies the balance between rejuvenation and mindfulness.

The Ghuraba Taxonomy

A holistic approach to your wellness and learning journey, integrating soul, self, intellect, and physique.

Ruh (Soul) — Spiritual Foundation ▼
  • Daily prayers, tazkir, and tafseer guidance
  • Reflection exercises to cultivate inner peace
  • Integration with Lighthouse activity books for spiritual enrichment
Nafs (Self/Emotions) — Emotional Wellness ▼
  • Guided self-reflection exercises
  • Emotional regulation and mindfulness activities
  • Understanding motivations and intentions for daily actions
Intellectual (Brain) — Knowledge & Learning ▼
  • Lighthouse activity books curated by Project Lead
  • Experiential learning: assembling, disassembling, observing
  • Ghuraba Taxonomy: Analyzing, Applying, Evaluating, Reflecting
Physique (Body) — Physical Wellness ▼
  • Fitness routines and movement activities
  • Nutrition-focused meal planning and diet suggestions
  • Wellness center integration: hands-on observation by Project Lead

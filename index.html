Our Heritage

The Hassan Choudhury Family Legacy & The Doctrine of Light.

A Riverine Sanctuary Built on 87.54 Bigha

MH Alo Chaya Limited is not a hotel. It is a "Lighthouse" (Ghuraba Al Manara), a sanctuary built on the banks of the Dhanagada river.

Our philosophy is rooted in the "Doctrine of Light," combining our family legacy with the holistic Ghuraba System—developing the Physique (kayaking, nature), the Qalb (serenity, heart), and the Ilm (intellect, "The Game").

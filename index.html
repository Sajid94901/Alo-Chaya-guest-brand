The Alo Chaya Experience

Alo Chaya is not just a resort—it’s a century of heritage, thoughtfully curated into a riverine luxury experience. Located just 2.5 hours from Dhaka, our 87-bigha estate is a sanctuary where you awaken to sunlight dancing on the water. The 60,000 sq ft main facility features spacious rooms crafted with teak and bamboo, each offering more space (≈354 sq ft) than a typical Dhaka 5-star hotel.

The Daroga’s Vision

The story of Alo Chaya begins with our great-grandfather, "The Daroga," whose vision led to the acquisition of this pristine riverine land, the foundation of the Fatima Mahal, and our family's deep connection to this place.

×

A Century of Community

Our maternal great-grandfather purchased the Sarkarbari land in 1912, establishing a century of deep community trust and local presence that is vital for our authentic, heritage-driven hospitality.

×

The Founder's Legacy

Our father, Mahmudul Hassan, is the founder who built the first 5-room bungalow in 2009 and the Mahmud Tower (our primary collateral) in 2016, turning a family retreat into the foundation for a world-class resort.

×

The Next Generation

As Project Lead, Sajid Hassan brings modern strategic planning and a commitment to operational excellence, ensuring this multi-generational legacy continues to thrive and innovate.

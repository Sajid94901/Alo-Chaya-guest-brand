The Daroga

The Daroga, paternal great-grandfather of Sajid Hassan, built Fatima Mahal at 19 North South Road, Siddique Bazar, old Dhaka in the early 1900s. He also owned the land at Enayet Nagar, Matlab, Chandpur, which he left as it was mainly a smuggling/transport hub at the time.

×

Mahmud Tower

Mahmud Tower was built by Mahmudul Hassan, the Founder, replacing the original Fatima Mahal on Siddique Bazar site. It stands as a modern testament to the family legacy and a primary collateral asset securing Alo Chaya.

Sajid Hassan

Sajid Hassan, the third son and current Project Lead, carries forward the century-long vision of Alo Chaya, blending heritage, strategy, and modern hospitality frameworks.

Asif Hassan

Asif Hassan, second son of the Founder Mahmudul Hassan, serves as 2nd Deputy Managing Director for Alo Chaya. He holds dual Higher Distinctions in Civil & Structural Engineering from UTS and leads structural and expansion projects.

Your riverine sanctuary awaits

Escape the city and discover a world where luxury meets a century of heritage on the serene banks of the Meghna River. Experience Alo Chaya. Alo Chaya is not just a resort — it's a century of heritage, thoughtfully curated into a riverine luxury experience. Located just 2.5 hours from Dhaka, our 87 Bigha estate is a sanctuary of space, privacy, and craft.

Heritage & Who We Are

The Daroga began this land’s story with Fatima Mahal. Mahmudul Hassan (the Founder) built the MM House and the original guest bungalow, later establishing Mahmud Tower at 19 Northsouth Road, Siddique Bazar. Today, Sajid Hassan — the founder’s 3rd son and project lead — lives on-site (second-floor quarters) to ensure our promise: we live the brand.

Project lead note: Sajid reinvests operationally and brings IP (The Lighthouse Tales), strategic planning, and hands-on execution to every guest stay.

Why Alo Chaya vs Others

Guests choose Alo Chaya because it balances proximity, scale, and intimacy. Competitors either overshoot on distance or underserve on space.

  • Dusai Resort & Spa (Moulvibazar) — Pricing: BDT 16,000++ to 100,000++; ~7 hours from Dhaka; remote and higher travel cost for short stays.
  • Kaalmegha (Gazipur) — Royal Villas ~BDT 38,450/day; Deluxe Villas BDT 22,450–24,650/day; limited event capacity.
  • The Cottage House — Small 2-bed villa; ~BDT 13,000–16,000; intimate but limited amenities.
  • The Cozy Nest — Deluxe 5,900–9,900; Premier Suite up to 12,900; Full Villa 28,000–39,000; boutique, budget-forward.
  • Sonargaon Royal Resort — Starts ~BDT 7,000++; conventional hotel experience.

Alo Chaya advantage: ~87 Bigha of riverine land, private lake, 60,000 sq.ft main facility, river immersion, curated wellness & gastronomy — and just ~2.5 hours from Dhaka.

Experience Alo Chaya

Alo Chaya is not just a resort—it’s a century of heritage, thoughtfully curated into a riverine luxury experience. Located just 2.5 hours from Dhaka, our 87-bigha estate is a sanctuary where you awaken to sunlight dancing on the water. The 60,000 sq ft main facility features spacious rooms crafted with teak and bamboo, each offering more space (≈354 sq ft) than a typical Dhaka 5-star hotel.

The Visionary: "The Daroga"

The story of Alo Chaya begins with our great-grandfather, "The Daroga," whose vision led to the acquisition of this pristine riverine land, the foundation of the Fatima Mahal, and our family's deep connection to this place.

Our father, the founder, translated a family vision into reality. He built the MM House and the original guest bungalow, laying the groundwork for the world-class resort that stands today. His work is the foundation of our legacy.

1. The Lighthouse Doctrine (The 'Why')

The core philosophy. Based on Haqq (Truth), Adl (Justice), and Ihsan (Excellence).

2. The Common Heart Doctrine (The 'How')

The rejection of the "idiot" academic models. Cites Sahih al-Bukhari 4425 (The Ship Parable) and Qur'an 24:30 (Lowering the Gaze) as guardrails against internal corruption. Defines the Ghuraba Doctrine (Strangers).

3. The Helix Model (The Structure)

The core organogram. Based on the Inversion of Authority (competence over title) and our "Broad Organisation" model. Mandates the "Hierarchy of Competent Tenure" (the "North" model).

6-8. Operational Ranks & Pillars (The Implementation)

The "HR Moral Auditor" (The "Bypass Authority")

The core "Maester" of the doctrine. This role has Director-level "Bypass Authority" to "Tackle" (Section 9) any member, at any level, for "Contextual" violations (like arrogance, or "False King" idiocy) based on the JiMini Window (Section 23). They wear the Grey Coat.

Ranks (The "Hierarchy of Competent Tenure")

Ranks are based *only* on the ACEM (Section 5) score.

  • Chief Versatilist (Head Maester): The top Specialist (200k Taka).
  • Deputy Versatilist (Operational Heir): The 2nd highest ACEM score (160k Taka).
  • "Versatilist" (88k Ideal): The *starting point* of the Versatilist track (88k Taka).
  • "Maester" (Specialist): Master of *one* domain (e.g., Head Room Coordinator) (65k Taka).
  • "Specialist" (Foundational): The standard competent Specialist (45k Taka).
  • "Trainee Specialist": The entry-level "Dignified Roll" (30k Taka).

Financial Pillars (The "Strong Believer" Model")

  • The Dignity Wage: Year 1 salaries based on competence, *not* tenure.
  • Mutual Crisis Fund: The "Common Heart" safety net.
  • Versatility Pay: The "Broad Organisation" model, rewarding "square footage" (skill variety).

9. Ground Theory: The Tackle Defense Protocol (TDM)

The core principle of tackling the "Enemy from Within" (nafs) first. This is the HR Moral Auditor's primary protocol, built to "Tackle" and retrain any "Maester Pycelle" (Section 4), "False King" (Section 4), or "False Mask" (Section 23) archetype.
